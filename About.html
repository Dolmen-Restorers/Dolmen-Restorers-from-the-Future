Let us introduce ourselves

Our names are not important, but our background is. In the 25th Century, things are very different to the timeline you know today. The world, facing destruction needed saved, and the world's top Sceientic research teams, came together, to find a way to save it. A device, a type of interspatial temporal displacement pod, was put together. It was recognised that the temporal distortions were a result of dolmen misalignment in the early 21st Cantury and the only way to protect the future, was to return to the past and make corrections.

Two brave men were selected from millions who were screened. They had the necessary braveness and foolishness to undertake such a task. The Dolmen Restorers from the Future had come into being.
