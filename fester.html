Welcome to the Dolmen Restorers

Welcome to the website of the Dolmen Restorers from the Future, your portal into a world of understanding and discovery.

The Cosmic alignment was off. The future had been badly disturbed, so from our Zone, in the 25th Century, it was necessary to travel time and space, to correct the dolmen alignment and synchronize the past and future.

This website will document our travels and is a log of our work.

Through a careful selection process, two brave temporalnauts were selected to journey to the past — which is your 21st Century.

Based in the 25th Century, and after years of training with the necessary instrumentation, they were dispatched to rectify the timeline.

We hope, through the explanations provided on this website, to give you an understanding of the need for dolmen restoration — and its vital role in protecting our heritage and future, as the two are interwoven in ways that most of us have failed to recognise.

Let the adventure in Time and Space commence today. A world of wonder awaits you.
